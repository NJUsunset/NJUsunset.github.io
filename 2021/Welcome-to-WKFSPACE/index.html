Something about the site

This site is a static blog established with hexo on Linux system (Ubuntu) and GitHub page service. The repository of this website can be found here. Please leave your comment in Issues Page if you’ve found any error of the site or got some suggestion.

  • Why Ubuntu other than Windows?

Though I use windows 10 as my major working and entertaining system, it really gets me mad when I try to install Git and Node.js into my windows. I can hard bare so many different shell windows other than CMD and PowerShell. For now I’ve got Git, Node.js, Anaconda and two versions of Python, plus Putty for ssh usage. And different terminals have isolated command. So I chose ubuntu for a union terminal, maybe just because I’m lazy. Considering convenience, Windows is absolutely the worst working environment.

  • Why GitHub page since I have VPS?

That’s simply because I’m lazy. I found tutorial originally on zhihu, and it was a tutorial based on hexo and GitHub. So……that the point.

The establish process of the blog will be later shared in my post category SHARE, so that you can refer to something if you want to set up your own space to write something. In fact, you need just very little knowledge about coding when setting up a site like this.

Something about the blog

The blog will be the main place to share pieces of my life. And most of the post will be written in English. Why? Personally I think it’s mainly a kind of the shy of mother tongue. As I do bad at composing words in Chinese, I’m unwilling to write my motions and my thoughts in Chinese. And another great problem is that switching between Chinese input and English is annoying (markdown marks are English punctuations and many work improperly with Chinese ones). But that also depends. Of cause I cannot write everything in English with current English level (planning for the CET-6).

Optimistically, the content of the blog will include my motions and thoughts, some notes on coding, some notes on physics (as I major in physics) and some notes on astronomy. Personally I think I’m a person who thinks more and talks much less, and I hardly find a comfortable place to talk. So I’ll try to talk in this space. And if you have something to say, you can leave you words in comment zone.(Currently I haven’t understand how to deal with these parts, so you may have to wait for a while.)

Something about myself

Objectively, I’m not a good writer, even in Chinese. I’ve been keeping a bad habit of thinking little when reading. Be lazy on thought leading to direct receiving other than evaluating. But as we all know, the info we receive isn’t always right since far early than we get into high school. Even something that much like to be the right things can be wrong. (Once one of my teachers said 70% of articles on NATURE are wrong) Considering “right” or “wrong” at this place, I’m talking about whether the proposition coexists harmoniously with experimental facts or empirical facts. So I’m trying to change it, trying to think critically. I’m planning to put this part into category THOUGHT.

And I plan to finish “myself” part later with diffrent posts.

And much thanks for your patience to read till here.

I say nothing but myself.
